About BCORES

Search for relevant conference proposals from journal articles

BCORES is a conference recommendation system designed for researchers to find the most suitable academic conferences for presenting their scientific papers. Using the title and abstract of a paper, the system employs natural language processing (NLP) and deep learning models to analyze and suggest conferences with high relevance.



BCORES takes into account various factors such as the weight of the title, abstract, and conference rankings, providing precise and efficient recommendations. By simplifying the search process, the system helps researchers save time and increase their chances of submitting to reputable and relevant conferences.
